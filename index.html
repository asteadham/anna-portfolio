About Anna

I am a mid-level UX researcher currently working for CareerBuilder.com, an agile technology environment. I am currently principal researcher for multiple products that are both business and consumer facing. In this role, I work with designers and product managers to deliver the best solution to users.

I gather frequent user insights and feedback via in-person and remote usability/concept feedback sessions, unmoderated online tests, surveys, voice of customer feedback and analytics data to inform business solutions.

My research experience includes designing, moderating, note-taking, recruiting and delivering findings for consumer, business and SaaS user studies. Much of this experience includes working on a team with fellow researchers and designers to achieve the best results possible.
