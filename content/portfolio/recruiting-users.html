PROBLEM

Lack of resources and time to recruit the correct user type for our usability testing.

SOLUTION

I utilized several tools including, a company Talent Network, scheduling software and multiple screeners to ensure we were bringing in the correct users for each of our job seeker usability sessions.

PROCESS

When I started the company as a User Experience Research intern, I was tasked with recruiting and scheduling many of the job seekers we brought in for usability testing or spoke with for our persona research. At the time I started, the only methods for recruiting our participants was posting research participant positions on our own site and ads on Craigslist. Since we had a large constraint on our budget, we were not able to utilize any outside recruiting firms.

I worked with many internal teams within the company to utilize the products we sell to attract more participants and establish better screening systems.

FINDINGS/OUTCOMES

Once implementing our new screener system and Talent Network community, I was able to easily search for job seekers based off of the specifications we needed for that usability test. This process is now being used by our research coordinator, in conjunction with the assistance of an outside recruiting firm.
